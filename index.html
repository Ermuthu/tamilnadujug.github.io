About Us

Tamilnadu Java User Group (TamilNadu JUG) is an active community that brings together professional developers, students, and professors to foster learning and collaboration around Java and modern software development. Unlike typical JUGs, we are inclusive of academia, ensuring the gap between industry and education is bridged.

What We Do

Meetups

We organize regular meetups across Tamil Nadu, covering modern Java topics and hands-on sessions.

Student Engagement

Encouraging students and professors to participate actively, ensuring practical exposure beyond classrooms.

Opensource

Support Opensource projects
